About Me

I am a south Florida native and went to the Univsersity of Miami for undergrad. I went on to get various jobs in the maritime industry, working as a marine survey technician and eventually got into ROV (remotely operated vehicle) piloting. I would travel to meet ships at various destinations to pilot and maintain ROVs, while conducting scientific research under the chief scientist for the cruise. I then had my share of traveling so decided to get a more land-based job and started working at Teledyne Marine as an applications engineer for acoustic modems. I am currently still in that role and my company is helping put me through a coding bootcamp so I can more thouroughly understand the modems, how they are programmed and how to help improve the technology overtime.

So far I only have a few projects to share but am excited to learn and create more along the coding bootcamp journey!
